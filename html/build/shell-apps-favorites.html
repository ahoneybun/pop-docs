Pin your favorite apps to the launcher

To add an application to the launcher for easy access:

  • Drag the application's icon from the dash onto the launcher.

  • Alternatively, when an application is running, select the Keep in launcher item in the application's Quick List.

The launcher icon order can be changed by dragging an icon off of the launcher, and then back onto it in the desired location.

To remove an application icon from the launcher, right click on the application icon and de-select Keep in Launcher.
