Screen problems

Most problems with the display are caused by issues with graphics drivers or configuration. Which of the topics below best describes the problem you are experiencing?

Change the size/rotation of the screen — Change the resolution of the screen and its orientation (rotation).How can I make the screen brighter? — Laptops and desktop monitors usually have buttons that you can press to change the brightness.The screen locks itself too quickly — Change how long to wait before locking the screen in the Screen settings.What is Unity 2D? — If your computer's graphics card doesn't support certain features, a more basic version of Unity will be started.Why do things look fuzzy/pixelated on my screen? — The screen resolution may be set incorrectly.Why does my screen go dim after a while? — When your laptop is running on battery, the screen will dim when the computer is idle in order to save power.Why won't my computer turn back on after I sent it to sleep? — Some computer hardware causes problems with suspend/hibernate.My computer will not turn on — Loose cables and hardware problems are possible reasons.
